The problem

Manual release checks create avoidable risk.

Teams often work across spreadsheets, QA documents, tickets, platform exports, and operator requests. Validora turns fragmented release checks into one structured pre-production review.

01

Hidden configuration gaps

Expected setup and actual platform configuration can silently diverge before launch.

02

Weak approval evidence

Sign-offs and operational decisions are often spread across documents and conversations.

03

Costly release incidents

One missed detail can delay a launch, trigger rework, or create revenue and compliance risk.

The platform

A safety layer before go-live.

Validora reviews release inputs, compares them against actual setup, scores the risk, and produces a clear decision for the release team.
